    • Audrey is a young portrait photographer who loves taking pictures of the world.

     

    Born in Accra, Ghana, she has always been surrounded by colorful human elements, flowery cultures and a lot of spicy food. She now lives in New Haven, CT where she studies Neuroscience at Yale University.

     

    In her photos, her goal is capture the softness in human interaction, attempting to make the most removed portraits relatable to. She uses a 35mm camera and produces black and white prints which she scans and uploads as part of her work.

     

    This year she is challenging herself with transitioning into color and digital photography to see and show the world in a different light. She hopes to make more images in New York and New Haven in this time.
